The Future of Tokenized Indices

LXY will represent a revolutionary approach to portfolio management - a single token providing exposure to a carefully curated basket of digital assets. Through decentralized governance and automated rebalancing, LXY aims to simplify portfolio diversification while maintaining complete transparency and user control.

Key Features

  • Automated Portfolio Rebalancing
  • Decentralized Governance
  • Transparent Asset Management
  • Community-Driven Development

Community Governed

  • Help shape the future of decentralized indices
  • Vote on index composition changes
  • Participate in rebalancing schedules
  • Guide protocol improvements

LXY is designed as a utility token for index management and DAO governance. It is not intended as an investment product. The token and associated protocol will be governed by the community through the LXY DAO. Participation may be restricted in certain jurisdictions.
